ENTERPRISES

Customers are realizing improved efficiencies, reduced costs, downtime and improved safety from their IoT deployments. And partners are playing a big part in customer success. The IoT market requires collaboration across the partner ecosystem, with each partner bringing the right technology or expertise to successfully deliver outcomes for each target industry.

The breadth of partners engaged in a typical solution delivery range from channel partners, system integrators, ISVs, cloud partners, to equipment builders. Customers also need integration and channel partners to help create, install and maintain these IoT solutions.

IoThings, through our XCELERATE Program, has established strong relationships with IT and OT product and service delivery businesses. Our capabilities help them grow their businesses, allowing them to extend their practices to serve your extended enterprise – securely connecting assets and managing the flow of data from the IoT edge to the enterprise applications.

Partner with IoThings to access a rich pool of "glocal" technology vendors and domain-specific service delivery partners to address your unique business challenges, augmented by opportunities to securely monetize your data globally.

GOVERNMENT

Government agencies are striving to deliver quality services in increasingly complex environments. And the public sector is also looking at ways to apply Internet of Things (IoT) technologies to find new value for citizens, aiming to enhance capabilities, streamline processes, and engage partners.

Central to successful IoT implementation in government is the ability to leverage an ecosystem of partners who can help provide a broad level of expertise and ensure you not only have the technological integration capabilities, but also the cultural and softer factors that are so important.

Governments needs to collaborate with industry to secure the jobs and economic growth opportunities associated with the rapid deployment IoT. And the main areas set to benefit from rapid deployment of IoT are public health, transportation, retail, logistics and worksite automation.
