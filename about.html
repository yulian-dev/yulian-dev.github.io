Our Indigenous Commitments

We would like to recognise the traditional owners across Australia and respectfully pay our respects to those Elders past, present and future.

Vision

To drive self-determination of Indigenous people throughout Australia by empowering them through jobs, industry and Government engagements within their nations.

As we drive to ensure that no person is left behind, RC Birli has focused on bridging the gap between the possible and desired. We do this by ensuring our commitment to local communities and valuing local partnerships, to ensure our First Nations people have access to the best experts and services teams.

We are also committing 2% of our company profits to providing jobs that contain education programs and scholarships that directly assist those children most at need, to allow them to embrace their thirst of knowledge and excel in the modern world. All of this is done whilst RC Birli maintains its connection to the land and those that have come before.

We understand that each is unique and because of this the way our pathways are offered to each should also be.
