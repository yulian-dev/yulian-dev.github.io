OUR COMPANY

Global IoT Solutions Aggregator and Value-Added Services company helping IT/OT Channel Partners drive Business Transformation initiatives across multiple industry verticals.

IoThings helps forward-looking businesses harness the power of the Internet of Things (IoT) with technology solutions and services that accelerate time-to-value and create sustainable business capabilities.

Leveraging a vendor portfolio of hardware and software components, solutions, innovative platforms, competencies and services for IoT across several industry verticals and applications, we enable forward-thinking businesses to achieve their desired business goals and outcomes.

Our role is to simplify, accelerate and optimize the development, deployment and operation of market-facing IoT solutions through a channel-led delivery model.

Channel partners can use our Solution Aggregation and/or Professional Services to resell, integrate or develop their in-house solutions themselves, or alternatively, utilize our Solution-as-a-Service capabilities to bring cutting-edge IoT solutions to market and service them over time. We also offer business and technology advisory services to help partners and their customers formulate IoT strategies, conceptualize solutions, go-to-market effectively, and harness enabling technologies for maximum impact.
