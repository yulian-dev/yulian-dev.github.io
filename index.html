Why we are coming together:

RC Birli acknowledges Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander People as the traditional Custodians of this land and we pay our respect to their Elders, past, present and emerging.

As a proud Indigenous, RC Birli is committed to elevating our First Nations people to becoming a leading infrastructure and service provider within the Australian marketplace. With a continued connection to land and country, we are a unique position to integrate emerging technologies into closing the Digital Divide that exists in our communities.

Smart Lighting

The creation of connected Smart Poles is a next generation design of combined technology to enable 5G infrastructure and capability to remote roads and rail routes across Australia.

Workforce Management

Facilitating specialist supply, hire and installation to the infrastructure and construction industries. Our equipment list can dry or wet hired to compliment construction, high voltage, lighting installation and maintenance projects.
